Zhuhai 🏫 (Oct. 2023)

From October 8th to October 31st, I engaged in academic exchanges at Zhuhai Campus of Sun Yat-sen University. Compared to the climate in Beijing, the weather in Zhuhai in October was very pleasant (except for the occasionally glaring midday sun). The campus is located right by the coastline, and I enjoyed riding my bicycle along the seaside, feeling the sea breeze, and listening to music. Although life was comfortable (despite the busy academic schedule), it was somewhat monotonous compared to Beijing. I believe that autumn in Beijing is the best season of the year, but it seems that with the end of October, winter has begun.

Datong 🛕 (Oct. 2023)

I haven’t traveled on the National Day holiday for many years. This time, we made enough preparation (psychologically) to travel to Datong, a city with a long history. Not surprisingly, a lot of people gathered here. We went to Yungang Grottoes, Yingxian Wooden Tower and Datong Museum. The intricate carvings and majestic Buddhas left me in awe, offering a glimpse into the artistic brilliance of ancient China. Besides, we also explored the Huayan Monastery, an architectural gem that echoed with whispers of dynasties long past. The intricate woodwork and serene courtyards offered a serene retreat from the bustling city.

Guilin ⛰️ (Aug. 2023)

After the social practice (Aug, 2023), we drove to Guilin (and also Yangshuo) to experience the famous Karst topography and Danxia landform (Danxia landform also shows in Rongxian). This is my second time to travel in Guilin, and we were surprised to found that the souvenirs in every tourist attraction have no difference compared with 10 years ago. Guilin, especially Yangshuo is really a beautiful city, however, how to manage the homestays, tourist attractions, local people and the construct the infrastructure are major problems. Honestly speaking, at the current stage, Guilin’s tourism cannot be described as sustainable. You cannot wait until the stalactites and stalagmites joining together several hundred years later and named it a new scenery spot.

Pasadena, Los Angeles 🎠🏖️🌆 (July. 2023)

We went to Pasadena to attend the IGARSS 2023 conference (July, 2023). I was really interested in the shows and lectures given by NASA. Beside the famous tourist attractions such as Santa Monica, Universal Studio Hollywood, LA downtown and The Getty Center, we went to the Griffith Observatory twice and saw the sunset there. We also used the telescope to see the Venus, which really looked like the moon in summer. Pasadena is a beautiful and small city compared to Los Angeles, especially after going to the LA downtown. We also found something that are “region-specific”, such as the Mario souvenirs. We bought the wristband and mushroom water bottle in the Universal Studio Hollywood and would like to show them in every taken photo. However, when we walked out of the amusement park, we felt that these things were out of place with the surrounding environment ᶘ ᵒᴥᵒᶅ

Yulin 🍜 (July. 2023)

We went to Yulin, Guangxi to participate in the social practice of doctoral students from June 25th to August 5th. During social practice, I chose to participate in the natural resource planning project of Rongxian County Forestry Bureau, Yulin City, Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, to gain an in-depth understanding of Rongxian’s efforts in green economic development, ecological protection and resource utilization. During the visit, I gained an in-depth understanding of the forest medicine resources in Rongxian County, the research and seed protection of the Forestry Science Research Institute, and the construction of small and micro wetlands, which gave me a deeper understanding of the development of the green economy. Compared to other cities such as Guilin or Liuzhou, Yulin is nearer to Guangdong province, therefore they have similar eating habitis. Besides, Rongxian is famous for its rice flour(?) and fruits such as Cinnamon Lychee(?) and Shatian Pomelo.

Zhuhai 🌊 (Apr. 2023)

Our research group went to Zhuhai to attend the The First Forum on AI Interdisciplinary Research (FAIR 2023) in April. At weekend, we visited the Zhuhai Lovers’ Road, Statue of Fisher Girl, and Zhuhai Opera House. We spent some time exploring the area around the Opera House, flying the UAV, and taking a city walk along the coastline. I really recommend the rice-soup based hot pot in Zhuhai, which is originally the famous food in Shantou. The seafood and beef were delicious boiled in rice soup. The most exciting thing for me was that I caught the livehouse performance of my favorite (currently, XMASWu ) rapstar. I really enjoyed it❗️

Jieyang 🍲 🛵 (Feb. 2023)

We went to Meizhou to attend a wedding party (Feb, 2023), after that, we spent one day and a half traveling in Jieyang. We tried some traditional (maybe not) food in Jieyang. It was … clean and hygienic, although the taste was not bad. My favourite part was riding a electromobile through the streets or along the river with friends. The wind blew, which was comfortable in a warm winter.

Chongli 🏂 (Feb. 2023)

I tried to learn snowboarding for the first time this winter. I stayed in Chongli for a week from Feb.1, 2023 ~ Feb.7, 2023 alone. It was difficult than I thought… My caudal vertebra, wrists and knees seemed broken. However, learning a brandly new skill is very interesting for me, and I am excited to seek for more opportunities to practice it!

Changsha 🥤(July. 2022)

After hearing the “Sexy Tea” for a long time, I thought I have to try it myself. Therefore, I went to Changsha on the first summer holiday during my Ph.D. study (2022, July) with my friends in middle school, who just finished her college study. (By the way, she learnt Russian, that is cool.) Thankfully it rained during those days, or we didn’t even want to go outside due to the boiling temperature!

Xi’an 🏯 (Apr. 2021)

I went to Xi’an with my 师兄师姐 of the research group during the last semester (2021, April) of my undergraduate study after I came back from Qingdao. They went there for work and I went there for fun (purely). This was my first time using a DJI UAV to record the scene in 大唐不夜城. We also rode bikes together on the ancient city walls.

Qingdao 🌊 (Apr. 2022)

In April 2021, I worked at the Pilot National Lab for Marine Science and Technology in Qingdao . I played around in Qingdao on Tomb-Sweeping Day with my Mom, she came to see me. However, that April was quite cold. It was really freezing that we stood by the sea, almost blown away by the wind. Anyway, there was not much memory and work was the most important at that time! We relaxed about one day and a half and then started to work again.

Guangzhou 🍨 (Jan. 2020)

We stayed in Guangzhou during the winter holiday in 2020, which was the time when covid-19 started. After that, I haven’t had long-distance travel until now (I mean 2022). Guangzhou is famous for its delicious cuisine. I even broke my stomach because I ate a lot every day, six meals per day. CHENTIANJI’s fishskin was the most delightful thing during this travel!

Argentina 🏔️🐧 (Jan. 2019)

While I had my winter school in BA in Janurary 2019, I visited some famous landscapes there.

IGUAZU was the very first place where I started my vlog. As a symbol in Happy Together, IGUAZU is a romantic place and have attracted many movie fans to visit (although I am not).

We went to El Calafate for the glacier. We hiked on the glaciers and even drank beers during the break, with ice obtained from nature. Seeing the iceberg collapse when sitting in the boat to go around them was really amazing.

USHUAIA is the southernmost part of the land in the Southern Hemisphere, which is also the place closest to the south pole. Many people traveling to the south pole will fly to USHUAIA first and then take a boat to the terminal. The residents there said it took about 40 hours to go to the south pole from USHUAIA. I love USHUAIA most because I saw the wild penguins, my favorite animals. Luckier than usual, during that winter (or summer in the Southern Hemisphere), a couple of emperor penguins come to the island. I didn’t even expect to see emperor penguins before! Different from other cities in Argentina such as BA or IGUAZU, many Chinese people, especially senior tours travelled to USHUAIA. Also, it was my first time to eat the King Crab, which was really really cheap and fresh!
