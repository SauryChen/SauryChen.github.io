We travel, always on the road. Here is a page I summarize all my travel videos (vlogs) since the first time I started recording. It will advance with the times, I mean both this 📝 and my 📹 skills.

Argentina 🏔️🐧 (Jan. 2019)

While I had my winter school in BA in Janurary 2019, I visited some famous landscapes there.

IGUAZU was the very first place where I started my vlog. As a symbol in Happy Together, IGUAZU is a romantic place and have attracted many movie fans to visit (although I am not).

We went to El Calafate for the glacier. We hiked on the glaciers and even drank beers during the break, with ice obtained from nature. Seeing the iceberg collapse when sitting in the boat to go around them was really amazing.

USHUAIA is the southernmost part of the land in the Southern Hemisphere, which is also the place closest to the south pole. Many people traveling to the south pole will fly to USHUAIA first and then take a boat to the terminal. The residents there said it took about 40 hours to go to the south pole from USHUAIA. I love USHUAIA most because I saw the wild penguins, my favorite animals. Luckier than usual, during that winter (or summer in the Southern Hemisphere), a couple of emperor penguins come to the island. I didn’t even expect to see emperor penguins before! Different from other cities in Argentina such as BA or IGUAZU, many Chinese people, especially senior tours travelled to USHUAIA. Also, it was my first time to eat the King Crab, which was really really cheap and fresh!

Guangzhou 🍨 (Jan. 2020)

We stayed in Guangzhou during the winter holiday in 2020, which was the time when covid-19 started. After that, I haven’t had long-distance travel until now (I mean 2022). Guangzhou is famous for its delicious cuisine. I even broke my stomach because I ate a lot every day, six meals per day. CHENTIANJI’s fishskin was the most delightful thing during this travel!

Qingdao 🌊 (Apr. 2022)

In *April 2021, I worked at the Pilot National Lab for Marine Science and Technology in Qingdao. I played around in Qingdao on Tomb-Sweeping Day with my Mom, she came to see me. However, that April was quite cold. It was really freezing that we stood by the sea, almost blown away by the wind. Anyway, there was not much memory and work was the most important at that time! We relaxed about one day and a half and then started to work again.

Xi’an 🏯 (Apr. 2021)

I went to Xi’an with my 师兄师姐 of the research group during the last semester (2021, April) of my undergraduate study after I came back from Qingdao. They went there for work and I went there for fun (purely). This was my first time using a DJI UAV to record the scene in 大唐不夜城. We also rode bikes together on the ancient city walls.

Changsha 🥤(July. 2022)

After hearing the “Sexy Tea” for a long time, I thought I have to try it myself. Therefore, I went to Changsha on the first summer holiday during my Ph.D. study (2022, July) with my friends in middle school, who just finished her college study. (By the way, she learnt Russian, that is cool.) Thankfully it rained during those days, or we didn’t even want to go outside due to the boiling temperature!

Chongli 🏂 (Feb. 2023)

I tried to learn snowboarding for the first time this winter. I stayed in Chongli for a week from Feb.1, 2023 ~ Feb.7, 2023 alone. It was difficult than I thought… My caudal vertebra, wrists and knees seemed broken. However, learning a brandly new skill is very interesting for me, and I am excited to seek for more opportunities to practice it!

Jieyang 🍲 🛵 (Feb. 2023)

We went to Meizhou to attend a wedding party (Feb, 2023), after that, we spent one day and a half traveling in Jieyang. We tried some traditional (maybe not) food in Jieyang. It was … clean and hygienic, although the taste was not bad. My favourite part was riding a electromobile through the streets or along the river with friends. The wind blew, which was comfortable in a warm winter.

Zhuhai 🌊 (Apr. 2023)

Our research group went to Zhuhai to attend the The First Forum on AI Interdisciplinary Research (FAIR 2023). At weekend, we visited the Zhuhai Lovers’ Road, Statue of Fisher Girl, and Zhuhai Opera House. We spent some time exploring the area around the Opera House, flying the UAV, and taking a city walk along the coastline. I really recommend the rice-soup based hot pot in Zhuhai, which is originally the famous food in Shantou. The seafood and beef were delicious boiled in rice soup. The most exciting thing for me was that I caught the livehouse performance of my favorite (currently, XMASWu) rapstar. I really enjoyed it❗️
